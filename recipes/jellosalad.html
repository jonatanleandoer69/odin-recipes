Jello Salad

Description

Jello salad recipe with midwestern roots and a true vintage flavor. Down below is the recipe for this cool whip, pineapple, cottage cheese, lime, jello salad.

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces lime gelatin
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup hot water
  • 8 oz crushed pineapple
  • 1 cup cool whip
  • 1 cup cottage cheese

Instructions

  1. Dissolve gelatin and sugar in hot water
  2. Stir i pineapple and chill
  3. Whipe lime mixture using mixer. Add cottage cheese and beat together
  4. Fold in whipped topping
  5. Pour in flat pan. Chill.
  6. Enjoy!
